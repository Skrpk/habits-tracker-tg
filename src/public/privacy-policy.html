Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Welcome to Habits Tracker. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

Data Collection

When you use Habits Tracker, we collect and store the following information:

Habit Data

  • Habit Names: The names of habits you create to track your progress
  • Streak Information: Your current streak counts and completion history
  • Completion Dates: Dates when you mark habits as completed
  • Skipped Days: Dates when you choose to skip a habit while maintaining your streak
  • Dropped Days: Dates when you mark a habit as not completed (streak reset)

User Preferences

  • Timezone: Your timezone preference for accurate reminder scheduling
  • Reminder Schedules: Custom reminder schedules you configure for each habit (daily, weekly, monthly, or interval-based)
  • Reminder Settings: Whether reminders are enabled or disabled for specific habits

Telegram Account Information

  • Telegram User ID: Your unique Telegram identifier, used to associate your data with your account
  • Conversation State: Temporary state information during multi-step interactions (e.g., habit creation, schedule configuration)

Note: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information beyond your Telegram User ID. We do not access your Telegram contacts, messages, or any other Telegram data.

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Habit Reminders: To send you automated reminders at your preferred times based on your custom schedules
  • Progress Tracking: To track and display your habit streaks, completion history, and progress over time
  • Feature Provision: To provide core habit management features, including creating, viewing, editing, and deleting habits
  • Analytics: To generate personalized analytics and visualizations of your habit tracking data
  • Service Improvement: To maintain and improve the functionality and reliability of the bot

Data Storage and Security

Storage Location

All your data is stored securely in our database using industry-standard security measures. We use Redis/Vercel KV for data persistence, which provides robust security and reliability.

Security Measures

  • Data is encrypted in transit using secure protocols
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized systems and processes
  • Regular security assessments and updates are performed
  • Data is backed up regularly to prevent loss

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use Habits Tracker. If you stop using the service, your data will be retained for a reasonable period to allow for account recovery, after which it may be deleted in accordance with our data retention policies.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties. Your data is used exclusively to provide the Habits Tracker service to you.

The only exception to this policy is if we are required to disclose information by law or in response to valid legal requests from law enforcement or regulatory authorities.

锔Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can view all your habit data through the bot's commands and analytics features
  • Deletion: You can delete individual habits or all your data at any time using the bot's delete functionality
  • Modification: You can update your habits, schedules, and preferences at any time
  • Opt-Out: You can stop using the bot at any time, and you can disable reminders for specific habits
  • Data Portability: Your data is associated only with your Telegram User ID, and you can request a copy of your data

How to Exercise Your Rights

  • Delete habits: Use the delete command in the bot
  • Update preferences: Use the bot's settings and configuration commands
  • Disable reminders: Configure reminder settings for individual habits
  • Request data deletion: Contact us through the bot or our support channels

Cookies and Tracking

Habits Tracker is a Telegram bot and does not use cookies or web-based tracking technologies. The web analytics page may use standard web analytics tools (such as Vercel Analytics) for service improvement purposes, but these do not collect personally identifiable information.

Children's Privacy

Habits Tracker is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete such information.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in servers located outside your country of residence. By using Habits Tracker, you consent to the transfer of your data to these servers. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notifying users through the bot when significant changes occur

Your continued use of Habits Tracker after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through:

  • The Habits Tracker Telegram bot
  • Our support channels (if available)

Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
